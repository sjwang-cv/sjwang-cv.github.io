Shijie Wang (王世杰)

The University of Queensland.

I am a Research Fellow at the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, The University of Queensland (UQ), Australia, working with Prof. Helen Huang. My research focuses on computer vision and multimodal foundation models, with particular interests in fine-grained recognition and retrieval, vision-language learning, and efficient image/video understanding.

I received my Ph.D. from Dalian University of Technology under the supervision of Prof. Haojie Li. Before that, I obtained my B.E. and M.S. degrees from the School of Software, Dalian University of Technology, in 2017 and 2020, respectively. I have also worked as a research intern at Huawei Cloud with Prof. Qi Tian, and collaborated with Prof. Wanli Ouyang at The University of Sydney.
